My Journey in e-Sports

When I was in China, I was a professional e-sports player in League of Legends. That experience is unforgettable for me. First of all, team events in e-sports, like traditional sports, also require a strong team understanding, and the importance of the team even surpasses some traditional team sports events. In one year of professional career, my interpersonal skills have been well exercised. Secondly, E-sports is about the hardworking spirit of turning impossible into possible. Keep refining myself, keep surpassing myself. In some cases, winning or losing is not important, what matters is whether I have made progress. E-sports has made me a strong-willed person. In daily life, I face difficulties actively and bravely instead of avoiding them. Last but not least, humility is also part of the esports spirit. No one can be invincible. GG means “good game” in E-sports. This is what an e-sports player will say to his opponent when he loses the game. Show respect to whom I can learn from his strength so that I can make continuous progress. What professional e-sports taught me I hope I can share with my audience, this is my idea for designing this theme.
